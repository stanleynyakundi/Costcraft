How to Use CostCraft for Construction Estimation

  1. Click on the Estimate dropdown menu.
  2. Select the Construction Cost page.
  3. Fill out the details of your construction project. If additional elements are needed, click Add Row. The total cost will be calculated and displayed in the Total Construction Cost cell at the bottom.
  4. Navigate to the Finishing Cost page and enter the finishing details. The calculated total finishing cost will be carried over and displayed in the Total Finishing Cost cell on the Construction Cost page.
  5. Go to the Building Services page and fill in the required details. Click the Calculate Total Cost button to see the total building services cost, which will also be reflected on the Construction Cost page.
  6. Click the Calculate Total Cost button at the bottom of the Construction Cost page to view the final project cost.
  7. For information about materials used in estimation, visit the Blogs page under the Resources tab.
  8. The Case Studies page showcases innovative construction methods used by engineers.
  9. For help with filling details in the tables, refer to the manual on the About tab .
  10. To check the value of different construction elements in Kenya, refer to the table displaying Average Building Material Prices.
